# Hawa Studios Style Selection Guide

This guide helps determine which Hawa Studios style is most appropriate based on the content and context of the music release.

## Wedding/Celebration Style
**Use when:**
- The album contains wedding songs or celebration music
- The release is a compilation of festive or ceremonial music
- The target audience is wedding planners or event organizers
- The artist specializes in traditional wedding performances

**Key visual indicators:**
- Elegant, luxury backgrounds (floral arrangements)
- Warm, celebratory atmosphere
- Formal attire, dignified posing
- Gold/ivory/white color palette

## Modern/Contemporary Style
**Use when:**
- The release is a new pop song, especially a remix
- The artist has a younger fan base
- The music incorporates modern production elements
- The release is targeting streaming platforms and social media

**Key visual indicators:**
- Dynamic compositions with engaging poses
- Bold color choices or strategic monochrome with accent
- Modern typography treatments
- Contemporary outfits and styling

## Traditional Portrait Style
**Use when:**
- The artist is established and well-known
- The music is classical Afghan in style
- The content is traditional or folkloric
- The release aims for a timeless, dignified presentation

**Key visual indicators:**
- Formal portrait composition
- Neutral, sophisticated backgrounds
- Formal attire (suits, ties)
- Clean, uncluttered design elements

## Performance/Entertainment Style
**Use when:**
- The release features live recordings
- The artist is known for theatrical performances
- The music is entertainment-focused rather than ceremonial
- The content references specific venues or performance contexts

**Key visual indicators:**
- Stage/venue settings
- Theatrical lighting elements
- Performance poses with microphones or instruments
- Dynamic interaction with audience or other performers

## Style Selection Process
1. Identify the primary purpose and audience for the release
2. Consider the artist's established image and previous covers
3. Match the music content to the most appropriate style category
4. Reference example covers from that category
5. Adapt the style prompts to match the specific requirements

## Special Considerations
- **Bilingual Releases:** For releases targeting both Afghan and international audiences, ensure both Dari/Pashto and English text are balanced appropriately
- **Series Consistency:** For volumes in a series, maintain style consistency with previous releases
- **Artist Branding:** Some artists have established a consistent look across releases - prioritize this over general style guidelines
- **Seasonal Content:** For holiday or seasonal releases, incorporate appropriate elements while maintaining the Hawa Studios aesthetic
