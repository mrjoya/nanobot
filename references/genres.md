# Genre Style Reference

Quick reference for genre-specific visual elements when crafting custom prompts.

## Electronic & Dance

| Genre | Visual Elements | Typography | Color Palette |
|-------|----------------|------------|---------------|
| **Synthwave** | Neon grids, sunset, chrome, DeLorean, palm trees | Chrome/neon outlined, retro | Purple, cyan, magenta, orange |
| **Electronic** | Abstract geometry, waveforms, circuits, glitch | Clean sans-serif, futuristic | Vibrant neons, gradients |
| **Lo-Fi** | Anime girl studying, cozy room, rain, vinyl | Soft sans, Japanese chars | Warm pastels, pink/blue |
| **Ambient** | Vast landscapes, clouds, space, water | Minimal, thin sans | Soft gradients, muted |
| **Trap** | Dripping effects, diamonds, money, smoke | Bold block letters | Dark with neon accents |

## Urban & Hip-Hop

| Genre | Visual Elements | Typography | Color Palette |
|-------|----------------|------------|---------------|
| **Hip-Hop** | Urban skyline, graffiti, chains, cars | Bold sans, 3D effect | Gold, black, red |
| **R&B** | Silk, intimate lighting, luxury | Elegant modern | Warm, sensual tones |

## Rock & Metal

| Genre | Visual Elements | Typography | Color Palette |
|-------|----------------|------------|---------------|
| **Metal** | Skulls, flames, gothic architecture, ravens | Blackletter, distressed | Black, red, silver |
| **Rock** | Electric guitar, stage lights, grit | Bold, impactful | High contrast |
| **Punk** | Collage, torn paper, safety pins | Cut-out letters, DIY | B&W with accent color |

## Acoustic & Traditional

| Genre | Visual Elements | Typography | Color Palette |
|-------|----------------|------------|---------------|
| **Jazz** | Saxophone, piano, smoke, nightclub | Elegant serif, script | Warm browns, gold, cream |
| **Classical** | Orchestra, baroque frames, instruments | Ornate serif | Deep reds, gold, navy |
| **Country** | Landscapes, boots, guitars, barns | Western, rustic | Earth tones, sunset |
| **Reggae** | Palm trees, lion, beach, sun | Rounded, friendly | Green, yellow, red |

## Pop & Indie

| Genre | Visual Elements | Typography | Color Palette |
|-------|----------------|------------|---------------|
| **Pop** | Glossy, playful shapes, confetti | Bold sans-serif | Bright, saturated |
| **Indie** | Polaroid, nature, minimalism | Handwritten, vintage serif | Muted, desaturated |

## Prompt Enhancement Tips

- Add **"album cover composition"** for proper framing
- Include **"legible typography"** when text matters
- Use **"no watermarks, print-ready"** for clean output
- Specify **"square format"** for streaming platforms
